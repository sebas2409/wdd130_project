SAFETY FIRST: YOUR GUIDE TO SAFE TOOL USE

👷‍♂️

Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) Guidelines: Always wear appropriate safety glasses, heavy-duty gloves, ear protection, and dust masks when operating machinery in the shop.

Safety Golden Rules:
1. Wear your PPE at all times.
2. Inspect tools for damage before connecting them to power.
3. Ask staff for help if you are unsure how to operate a machine.
4. Never force a tool to do a job it wasn't designed for.
